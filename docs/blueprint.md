# **App Name**: PataMalaria

## Core Features:

- Secure User Accounts: Registration and login for Community Health Workers, Doctors, Health Officers, and Administrators, with role-based access control.
- Malaria Case Reporting Form: An intuitive interface for Community Health Workers and Doctors to submit new malaria incident details including patient data, symptoms, test results, and geographic location.
- Case Management Dashboard: A centralized dashboard allowing Health Officers and Doctors to view, update, and manage all submitted malaria cases and their statuses.
- Geospatial Incident Mapping & Analytics: Display malaria incidents on an interactive map, providing an overview and enabling data analysis by region or time, with an AI tool to highlight emerging hotspots.
- Automated Alert & Notification System: An AI tool that monitors reported cases to detect and automatically alert relevant health authorities about unusual surges in malaria incidents in specific areas.
- Real-time Statistical Reports: Generate dynamic daily, monthly, and regional statistical reports on malaria incidence, treatment outcomes, and trends for informed decision-making.
- Treatment and Prescription Tracking: Doctors can easily record, view, and update prescribed treatments for individual patient cases directly within the system.

## Style Guidelines:

- The chosen light color scheme is designed for clarity and a professional look. The primary color is a vital green (#29CC52), representing health and vitality. This versatile hue anchors the design. The background color is a heavily desaturated variant of the primary green (#E0F9E5), ensuring a clean, uncluttered visual space. The accent color is a more earthy green (#75BF3D), creating an analogous contrast to highlight interactive elements and provide depth without overpowering the design.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, neutral, and highly readable qualities, suitable for clear presentation of medical data and reports.
- Utilize a consistent set of clean, simple, and vector-based icons, such as those found in Material Design, to maintain a professional and easily understandable user interface.
- Implement a responsive, grid-based layout to ensure optimal display and functionality across various devices, from mobile phones to desktop computers. The design prioritizes clear segregation of information sections, intuitive navigation, and prominent display areas for data visualizations and reporting.
- Incorporate subtle and swift animations for interactive elements, dashboard updates, form submissions, and loading states to provide immediate feedback to the user without causing distractions or delays. Focus on efficiency and smooth transitions.
